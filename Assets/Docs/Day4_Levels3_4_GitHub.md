## New Gameplay Systems

- Security Keycard pickup
- Energy Core pickup
- EMP Device pickup
- Limited EMP charges
- Press F to fire EMP and stun guards
- Exit zones can require specific items before allowing scene transition

## Level Progression

1. MainScene: one mirror puzzle
2. Level02: two mirror puzzle
3. Level03: keycard, EMP device, guard, three mirrors
4. Level04: two energy cores, master keycard, two guards, four mirrors
