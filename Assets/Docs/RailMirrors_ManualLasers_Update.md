# Rail Mirrors and Manual Laser Switch Update

Changes:

1. Mirrors now move on visible rail tracks.
   - Stand near a mirror.
   - Hold Z / C to slide it along the rail.
   - Use Q / E to rotate it.
   - The rail turns green when the mirror is close to its intended alignment point.

2. From Level 2 onward, lasers start turned off.
   - Stand near the red switch.
   - Press X to activate/deactivate the laser emitter.

3. Maps from Level 2 onward are enlarged to give the rail-mirror puzzles more room.
