# Expert Level 5 and 6 Refinement

This patch refines Level 5 and Level 6 in four ways:

1. Larger layouts with cleaner zig-zag laser routes.
2. More deliberate decoy mirror placement, especially near side branches.
3. Additional blockers used as navigation pressure instead of random clutter.
4. Harder exploration pressure through extra guards and more spread-out pickups.

Level 5:
- 5 required reflections.
- Larger map footprint.
- 2 guard patrol routes.
- Pickups split across opposite quadrants.

Level 6:
- 6 required reflections.
- Largest map footprint.
- 3 guard patrol routes.
- Pickups spread across distant sub-areas.
- More decoy mirrors and corridor blockers.
