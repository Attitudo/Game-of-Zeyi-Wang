# Gate Side Ground Fix and Story Concept

Fix:
1. Side fill blocks around the gate are extended so their bottoms touch the floor.
2. Gate room side walls and back walls are extended to the floor.
3. Added small bottom skirt blocks to seal remaining visible side gaps.

Story:
The player is an apprentice light engineer trapped inside the Mirror Vault, an underground dungeon that once stored a powerful crystal called the Sun Core. The security system has gone rogue after the crystal was stolen. To escape, the player must restore light routes, reflect beams through ancient mirrors, avoid security guards, and power each receiver to unlock sealed gates. Each level represents a deeper chamber of the vault, ending in the final control room where the player reactivates the Sun Core and escapes before the dungeon locks down forever.
