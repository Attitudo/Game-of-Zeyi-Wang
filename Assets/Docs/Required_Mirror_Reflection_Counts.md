# Required Mirror Reflection Counts

This update adds an exact mirror-count rule to the receiver.

Rule:
- The receiver will only power on if the laser has reflected from the required number of unique mirrors.
- Too few mirrors will not open the door.
- Too many mirrors will also not open the door, so decoy mirrors remain misleading choices.

Required counts:
- Level 1: exactly 1 unique mirror
- Level 2: exactly 2 unique mirrors
- Level 3: exactly 3 unique mirrors
- Level 4: exactly 4 unique mirrors
- Level 5: exactly 5 unique mirrors
- Level 6: exactly 6 unique mirrors

This makes the puzzle difficulty increase by level and prevents direct/shortcut solutions.
